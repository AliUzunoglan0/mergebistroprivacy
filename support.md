Frequently asked questions
I bought gems / Remove Ads but didn't receive them. Open the in-game Shop and tap Restore purchases. If that doesn't resolve it, email us with your purchase receipt (from the App Store) and the approximate date and time of purchase.

How do I remove ads? Open the Shop from the home screen and purchase Remove Ads. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all ads.

How do I reset my game progress? Go to Settings → Reset progress inside the app. This cannot be undone.

I found a bug or have a suggestion. Email us at the address above with your device model, iOS/Android version, and a description of what happened — screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.
