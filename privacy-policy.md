# Privacy Policy — Merge Bistro

**Effective date:** July 8, 2026
**Developer:** Ali Uzunoğlan ("we", "us")
**Contact:** a.uzunoglan44@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the mobile game **Merge Bistro**
(the "App") handles on Android and iOS, and the choices you have.

## Summary

- Merge Bistro is an offline game. **We do not operate any servers and we do
  not collect, store, or sell your personal data ourselves.**
- Your game progress is saved **only on your device**.
- The App shows ads through **Google AdMob** and offers optional in-app
  purchases through **Google Play** and the **Apple App Store**. These
  third parties process limited device data as described below.

## Data we collect

We collect **no personal data**. The App does not require an account, does
not ask for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or microphone, and
does not transmit your game progress anywhere.

Game progress (board state, currencies, achievements, settings) is stored
locally on your device. Deleting the App deletes this data. You can also
reset it any time via **Settings → Reset progress** in the App.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show interstitial and rewarded ads. To serve
and measure ads, Google may collect and process device information such as:

- Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA, where
  permitted)
- IP address and coarse location derived from it
- Device and app information (model, OS version, app package name)
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks, crashes related to ads)

This processing is governed by Google's privacy policy:
<https://policies.google.com/privacy>
How Google uses data from partner apps:
<https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites>

**Your choices:**

- **European Economic Area / UK:** on first launch you will be asked for
  consent via Google's consent form (UMP). You can decline personalized
  ads; ads will then be non-personalized. You can change your choice later
  from the App's settings.
- **iOS:** the App asks for tracking permission via Apple's App Tracking
  Transparency prompt. If you decline, the advertising identifier is not
  used for personalized ads.
- **Android:** you can reset or delete your Advertising ID in
  *Settings → Google → Ads*.

## In-app purchases

The App offers optional purchases (gems, remove-ads, starter pack).
Payments are handled entirely by **Google Play** or the **Apple App
Store**; we never see or store your payment details, name, or address.
Purchase receipts are processed on-device to unlock content. Refunds are
subject to the store's policies.

## Children

Merge Bistro does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads
are served through Google AdMob with content ratings appropriate to the
App's store rating. If you believe a child has provided personal data
through a third-party service used by the App, please contact us and we
will assist with its removal.

## Data retention

We retain nothing, because we collect nothing. Third-party retention (ad
identifiers processed by Google) is described in Google's policy linked
above.

## Your rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar laws)

Since we do not hold your personal data, requests for access, deletion, or
portability of ad-related data should be directed to Google via the links
above. For anything else, contact us at the email below and we will help
within 30 days. Residents of California and the EEA/UK have the right to
know, delete, and opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information; we
do not sell personal information.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy (for example when adding analytics or new ad
networks), we will update this page and the effective date above. Material
changes will be noted in the App's store listing release notes.
